    Śrīraṅgam, Raṅganātha temple, no date given

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2024-04-24Z

    Editors:   Renato Dávalos and Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSSII2400182

    Summary: List of provisions for worshipping an image of the deity, including the recitation of Śrīpurāṇam.

    Hand Description:

    According to epigraphist, characters seem to be from ca. 11th century.
