    Translation by Emmanuel Francis

    1Prosperity! Fortune!

    1Fourty-eighth year of the glorious king Kulottuṅga Cōḻa, the emperor of the three worlds, alias the king Rājakesarivarman,

    1who ... [meykkīrtti of Kulottuṅga I Cōḻa].

    1[There are] five kācus ...

    1With these five kācus, we ...

    1-2...
