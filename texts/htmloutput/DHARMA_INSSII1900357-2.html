    4 ka⟨ṭa⟩va ◇ SR; kava VG

    Commentary

    The record contains no internal date. It might be dated to the year 14 of Uttamacōḻa, which is the internal date of SII 19.357-1, that Srinivasa Rao, Sircar, and Gai 1967 consider to be the first part of the present record.

    Bibliography

    Reported in ARIE 1928-1929 (ARIE/1928-1929/B/1928-1929/164).

    Edited in Srinivasa Rao, Sircar, and Gai 1967 (SII 19.357, lines 41-82). For lines 1-41, that Srinivasa Rao, Sircar, and Gai 1967 consider to be the first part of the present record, see inscription SII 357-1. Edited in Gillet 2023, based on autopsy and photos.

    Translated in Orr 2000: pages 116–118.

    This edition by Renato Dávalos and Emmanuel Francis (2024), based on the previous editions and on photos (Valérie Gillet, 2018).
