    Translation by Renato Dávalos and Emmanuel Francis

    1 Prosperity! Fortune!

    6-7 27th year of the glorious Lord Rājādhirāja, alias king Rājakesarivarman.

    1-6 who, ... [meykkīrtti of king Rājādhirāja I]

    7-10 To whomever kingsmen of Kōṉēriṉmaikoṇṭāṉ,

    • having said that "We have given, as ritual (nimittya) provisions (tāṉa), for [the benefit] of all our people, to Ampalattāṭi Tirunāvukkaraiyaṉ and his lineage, who are the reciters of holy hymns (tiruppatiyam viṇṇappam ceyum) at/for the tēvāram↓1 of the monastery of the temple [dedicated to] Mahādevar in Tiruvaṭakuṭi within the Uraiyūr-kūṟṟam in Kēraḷāntaka-vaḷa-ṇāṭu"
    • and having said that "We commanded (coṉṉām) to take (koḷḷa) one tūṇi of paddy daily for the enjoyment (pōkam-āka) of the monastery (maṭam) from the treasury of this god."
    We have given [so], according to [our] royal command (tirumukap paṭi), which he, among respected men/brahmins (aiyariṭaiyil), previously had (peṟṟuṭaiya).

    10-11 So that

    • he makes the endowment (nivantaṅkaḷ ceyyavum) to the temple servants (nivantakkāṟar) who perform various services [in the temple], at the aforementioned rate (coṉṉa vaṇṇamē),
    • he rotates (māṟṟavum) and appoint (iṭavum) the endowments and the temple servants who perform different services, including the Śivabrāhmaṇas of this god,
    • he obtains (peṟuvāṉ) [this],
    thus We commanded (coṉṉōm).

    12-14 We commanded (coṉ[ṉōm]) that he, among respected men/brahmins (aiya(riṭai)[yil]), obtains [...] according to [our] royal command ((tirumuka)p paṭiyē) [...].

    13-14 [This is the] signature of [...].

    14 In the year 37, [day] 311.

    14 [This is under the protection of] the Paṉmāheśvaras.
