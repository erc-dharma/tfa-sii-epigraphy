    Translation by Renato Dávalos and Emmanuel Francis

    1 Prosperity! Fortune!

    1-6 Meykkīrtti of king Rājādhirāja I...

    6-7 This is the year 27 of the glorious Lord Rājādhirāja, alias king Rāja-kēcari-paṉma.

    7-10 We have given, according to the royal command (tirumukappaṭi), which previously had him, along with [other] respected men/brahmins (aiyariṭaiyil), as the recipient (peṟṟuṭaiya) [of the endowment] that said:

    • saying “We have given, as ritual (nimittya) provisions (tāṉa), for [the benefit] of all our people, to Ampalattāṭi Tirunāvukkaraiyaṉ and his lineage, who are the reciters of holy hymns (tiruppatiyam viṇṇappam ceyum) at the tēvāram of the monastery of the temple [dedicated to] Mahādevar in Tiruvaṭakuṭi within the Uraiyūr-kūṟṟam in Kēraḷāntaka-vaḷa-ṇāṭu [that belongs to] Kōnerimaikoṇṭāṉ”;
    • and also saying “We commanded (coṉṉām) to take (koḷḷa) one tūṇi of paddy daily for the maintenance (pōkam-āka) of the monastery (maṭam) from the treasury of this god.

    10-11 We commanded “He, the recipient (peṟuvāṉ), shall

    • make the endowment (nivantaṅkaḷ ceyyavum) to the temple servants (nivantakkāṟar) who perform various services [in the temple], at the aforementioned rate (coṉṉa-vaṇṇamē),
    • rotate (māṟṟavum) and appoint (iṭavum) the endowments and the temple servants who perform different services, including the śaiva brahmins of this god.”
