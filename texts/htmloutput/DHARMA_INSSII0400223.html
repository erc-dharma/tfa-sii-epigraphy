    Citamparam, Nāṭarāja temple, time of Rajendra Cōḻa I, year 24

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2024-03-13Z

    Editors:   Renato Dávalos and Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSSII0400223

    Summary: List of expenses given for the Tiruvātirai festival celebrated in Chidambaram. The inscription includes donations of gold coins ( kācu ) to śrīmaheśvaras, reciters of Tiruttoṇṭattokai, weavers, oil sellers, merchants, among others.

    Hand Description:

    According to epigraphist, from line 7 ( takkaṇalāṭamum ), “the wording is quite differente from that of the other published inscriptions”.
